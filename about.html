Relax in
Our Luxury Hotel

Sea Palace is a Deluxe luxury retreat, beside the picturesque town of Ramanattukara, in the outskirts of Calicut. Nestled in a picture-perfect locale, amidst an ambience of absolute serenity, Sea Palace is a haven of leisure for the city-weary soul. A short 10 km drive away from Airport, the resort is close to the city, yet far removed from its din and bustle.
